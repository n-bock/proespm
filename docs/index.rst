- Processing of electrochemical, spectral and microscopical measurement files with optional import of Excel labjournals
- Create interactive, explorable, and multi-method HTML reports for each sample (approx. one report per measurement day)
- Practical integration in everyday lab routine and ease of use
- Conversion of binary data files to txt files for further analysis (Jupyter Notebook, QtiPlot etc.)


Supported Methods
-----------------
* **Microscopy:** optical, electron or scanning tunneling microscopy (also in electrochmical environments) and atomic force microscopy
* **Electrochemistry:** cyclic voltammetry, chronoamperometry, impedance spectroscopy
* **Spectroscopy:** x-ray photoelectron or raman spectroscopy
